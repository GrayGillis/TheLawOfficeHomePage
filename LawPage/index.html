The Law Offices of Soraya Rashid Gillis


Soraya Rashid Gillis provides legal services and business advice, with particular expertise in technology transactions. She not only advises clients regarding licensing and distribution models, pricing structures, business development, and strategic partnerships, she is also available to structure and negotiate all types of technology transactions to successful completion.

Soraya has been practicing law for more than 20 years. Prior to founding the Law Offices of Soraya R. Gillis in 2006, she was Associate General Counsel at Palm, Inc. Before joining Palm in 1998, Soraya was one of the early members of the Technology Transactions Practice Group at Wilson, Sonsini, Goodrich & Rosati. Soraya began her career as a corporate associate at O'Melveny & Myers in Century City.

Soraya received her law degree from the UCLA School of Law in 1993 (Order of the Coif). Soraya received her B.S. degree (With Great Distinction) in Business Administration (Finance) from San Jose State University.
